TRADITIONAL PIPING SYSTEM

The system currently used works, but is still innefficient, costly, and also time-consuming.
It requires 3 seperate components which causes an increase in installation times, as well as higher chances for installation errors.

By using the RiserPro, you can save time on the job, which saves you money.

WHY IS IT INEFFICIENT?

• Time-Consuming Setup: Firestop, riser clamp and barrier installed separately, increasing labor time and complexity.

• Bulky and Heavy Riser Clamp: More expensive to ship and transport, adding logistical challenges.

• Not Locally Manufactured: Longer lead times and higher costs due to supply chain dependencies.

• Weaker Materials: 0.04mm galvanized steel (250 MPa) prone to damage and corrosion.

• Frequent Maintenance: Shorter lifespan leads to higher replacement costs.

• Environmental Concerns: Galvanizing process produces VOC emissions, making it less sustainable.

Features & Benefits

• Cost Effective & Efficient:
RisePro is 50% more cost-efficient than traditional setups, thanks to its pre-installed firestop and streamlined manufacturing process. Reduce overall project costs while enhancing performance.

• Eco-Friendly & Safe:
Manufactured without VOC emissions, All Clamp Pro is a sustainable solution that meets modern environmental and safety standards. Say goodbye to the issues of galvanized steel and outdated installation processes.

• Superior Strength & Durability:
Built with 0.6mm stainless steel (450 MPa strength), All Clamp Pro offers exceptional resistance to wear, corrosion, and impact—ensuring a longer-lasting and more reliable support system.

• Integrated Firestop for Seamless Installation:
The firestop is factory-installed within the riser clamp, eliminating the need for separate on-site installation. This ensures faster setup, reduced labor costs, and a more secure fire protection system.

eliminating needless materials and fits in the space effectively without additional make-fit work.
